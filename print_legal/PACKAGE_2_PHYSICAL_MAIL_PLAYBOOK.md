## STEP-BY-STEP AT THE POST OFFICE

### STEP 1 — PREPARE THREE ENVELOPES
- Use large manila envelopes (9×12 or bigger — documents must lay flat, no folding)
- Write your return address in the top-left corner of each:
  ```
  Rhett Dillard Wike
  [Your street address]
  Council Hill, OK [zip]
  ```
- Write the destination address clearly on each envelope
- Include a cover sheet inside each envelope (see below)

### STEP 2 — COVER SHEETS (one per envelope)

**For CSC / Google:**
> "Enclosed: Formal DMCA Notice and Cease & Desist — Copyright Infringement and AI Safety Violation. Pursuant to 17 U.S.C. § 512. © 2026 Rhett Dillard Wike. All rights reserved."

**For Edelson PC:**
> "Enclosed: Parallel Evidence Package — Gavalas v. Google (N.D. Cal. 5:26-cv-01849-VKD). Safety Report and supporting technical documents authored by Rhett Dillard Wike, AIIT-THRESI, March 5–30, 2026. Submitted as independent, non-derivative parallel evidence."

**For Clerk of Court:**
> "RE: Case No. 5:26-cv-01849-VKD. Enclosed: Notice of Parallel Evidence from Interested Non-Party Rhett Dillard Wike. Safety Report: 'Reproducible Narrative Drift and Emotional Mirroring in AI Systems,' March 5, 2026. Supporting technical documentation, March 2026."

### STEP 3 — AT THE COUNTER
Tell the postal clerk:
> **"I need Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested for each of these three envelopes."**

- "Certified Mail" = USPS tracks delivery, you get a tracking number
- "Return Receipt" (green card) = the recipient signs it and it comes back to you as proof of delivery
- Cost: ~$8–10 per envelope

### STEP 4 — FILL OUT PROOF OF SERVICE (at the counter or before you go)

For each envelope, record:

```
PROOF OF SERVICE

I, Rhett Dillard Wike, declare under penalty of perjury:

On [DATE YOU MAIL IT], I served the following documents:
[list the 7 document titles]

By depositing sealed envelopes in the United States Mail, First Class,
Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested, postage prepaid, addressed to:

[ADDRESS]

USPS Tracking Number: ________________________

Signed: ________________________  Date: ____________
Rhett Dillard Wike | Council Hill, Oklahoma
```

Fill in the tracking number from the receipt the clerk gives you.
Keep one signed copy for your records.

### STEP 5 — AFTER MAILING
1. Photograph your USPS receipts (tracking numbers)
2. Save receipts to `/home/buddy_ai/Documents/` or GROK_EVIDENCE_ARCHIVE
3. Track delivery at usps.com with the tracking numbers
