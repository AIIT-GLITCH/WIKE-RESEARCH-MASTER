  Right number. Wrong system.
  AIIT-THRESI | March 28-29, 2026
=====================================================

THE CLAIM:

  "Quantum coherence in water lasts femtoseconds.
  Therefore water cannot support quantum effects
  relevant to biology, which operates on millisecond
  timescales."

=====================================================
  WHAT'S TRUE
=====================================================

  Individual water molecules in BULK water lose
  quantum superposition in ~10^-15 seconds at room
  temperature. This is measured. This is real.

  Hydrogen bond rearrangement in bulk water: ~1
  picosecond. Also measured. Also real.

  If you put a qubit in a glass of water on a table,
  it will decohere in femtoseconds. True.

=====================================================
  WHAT'S NOT TRUE
=====================================================

  The claim that this applies to ALL water in ALL
  configurations in ALL biological contexts.

  It doesn't. Because biology doesn't use bulk water
  for quantum effects. It builds structured water
  and does quantum in THAT.

=====================================================
  WHAT BIOLOGY ACTUALLY USES
=====================================================

  1. COLLECTIVE MODES (not single molecules)
     Water's hydrogen bond network oscillates
     collectively at ~1 THz.
     Underdamped oscillation: 170 femtosecond period.
     Science Advances (2020): "Energy transfer within
     the hydrogen bonding network of water following
     resonant terahertz excitation"
     These are network effects. Not single-molecule.

  2. GROTTHUSS PROTON WIRES (correlated tunneling)
     Protons hop along H-bond chains via quantum
     tunneling. The chain is the wire. Correlated.
     Not limited by single-molecule decoherence.
     PMC10475958 (2023).

  3. EXCLUSION ZONE WATER (different phase)
     Pollack: EZ water extends hundreds of micrometers.
     Liquid crystalline. Hexagonal sheets. Charge
     separated. NOT bulk water. Different phase.
     Different physics.

  4. DEBYE-SHIELDED WATER (extended timescales)
     Ion layers around structured water inside
     microtubules extend decoherence from femtoseconds
     to MILLISECONDS or longer.
     Wiest (2025), Oxford.

  5. FROHLICH CONDENSATION (self-organizing)
     Energy condenses into lowest vibrational mode.
     The OPPOSITE of thermal decoherence. Energy
     ORGANIZES instead of dispersing.
     Pietruszka (2025): in DNA, in water, at room temp.

  The femtosecond number was right for bulk water.
