The D.G. consent decree is actually EVIDENCE for the new case:

4. **The state began backsliding within months of release** — showing it WON'T do it voluntarily
5. **Children are now in Airbnbs and offices** — showing the harm is active and current

This is a STRONGER case than D.G. was in 2008 because:
- The state can't claim ignorance (13 years of monitoring)
- The state can't claim inability (they achieved compliance)
